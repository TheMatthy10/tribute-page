Felix Kjellberg

Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg is an England-based Swedish YouTuber, comedian, gamer, and philanthropist, known primarily for his Let's Play videos and comedic formatted shows. After registering his eponymous YouTube channel in 2010, Kjellberg primarily posted Let's Play videos of horror and action video games.

His channel experienced substantial growth in popularity over the next two years and he reached 1 million subscribers in July 2012, and now has over 100 million subscribers and 4100 videos. Over time, his style of content diversified to include vlogs, comedy shorts, formatted shows and music videos.
